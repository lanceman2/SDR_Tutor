Signal Dimensions

In SDR (software defined radio) we tend to used two dynamical variables to describe our radio signal, whatever that is. As a dynamicist we must ask ourselves why two variables, why not one, or maybe three. The choose of the number of dynamical variables must have a reason. As a physicist we must see this number of dimensions as the solution to an optimization problem, it can't just be arbitrary.

We'll look at two approaches to finding the number of dimensions. First we'll consider looking at the wave signal as a some kind of indirect measurement at one point in space of an induced electric potential due to the point being in proximity to an electromagnetic field.
